New COVID-19 cases by day scaled to population

Select countries you wish to comapare and the axis paramters. The graph shows the number of new confirmed COVID-19 cases on a specific date. The number of cases can be shown as total or per capita. The date can also be represented as a number of days passed since the first recorded COVID-19 death (negative numbers before that date) or a number of days passed since the government imposed lockdown (for countries without lockdown median date is taken). Specifics of the lockdowns can be found along with the country info (as links).
